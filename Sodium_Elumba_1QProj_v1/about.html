Who We Are

We are a group of students from Philippine Science High School advocating for the lowering of the age of criminal liability in the Philippines. Our group was formed after learning about the increasing cases of heinous crimes committed by youth who face minimal accountability due to the current law.

Motivated by real-life tragedies such as the Sophia Marie Coquilla case, we believe in balancing justice and rehabilitation for minors. Our mission is to educate the public, support victims, and encourage reforms in juvenile justice.
